The STRETCH System

Backed by our parent company STRETCH, we deliver ceiling solutions that combine manufacturing excellence with precision installation. Each system is engineered for longevity, performance, and visual impact.

Seamless Installation

Proprietary track-and-membrane system creates perfectly smooth surfaces up to 50m² without visible seams. Installation completed in days, not weeks.

Technical Integration

Accommodates lighting, HVAC, sprinklers, and AV systems. Modular access panels allow maintenance without compromising aesthetics.

Performance Standards

Fire-rated materials (B-s2, d0). Antimicrobial treatments available. 10-year warranty on materials and installation quality.

Professional Installation Process

Our certified teams deliver flawless results through systematic methodology and quality control at every stage.

01

Site Assessment & Measurement

Laser-precise measurements and structural evaluation. We identify integration points for lighting, HVAC, and other building systems before fabrication begins.

02

Custom Fabrication

Membranes manufactured to exact specifications in our STRETCH facility. Each piece is individually tagged and quality-inspected before delivery.

03

Track Installation

Perimeter tracks installed with millimeter accuracy using laser levels. Track system engineered to hold up to 15kg/linear meter tension load.

04

Membrane Tensioning

Controlled heating and precision tensioning creates perfectly smooth surface. Process completed in hours with minimal disruption to operations.

05

Quality Verification

Final inspection ensures perfect tension, seam integrity, and system integration. Documentation package includes as-built drawings and maintenance guidelines.
