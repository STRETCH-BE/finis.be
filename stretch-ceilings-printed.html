Why Printed Stretch Ceilings

Brand Environment Creation

Wayfinding Integration

Artistic Freedom

Backlit Compatible

Three Print Technologies

Select the printing method that aligns with your design intent and lighting strategy.

Opaque Printing

Full-Coverage Graphics

Standard UV printing on white PVC base. Maximum color saturation and contrast. Ideal for photorealistic imagery, brand graphics, and bold patterns viewed under ambient or accent lighting.

  • 100% Opacity coverage
  • 6-color Process printing
Translucent Printing

Backlit Graphics

Special translucent inks designed for LED backlighting. Graphics become luminous features that transform space atmosphere. Perfect for dramatic architectural statements and ambient lighting integration.

  • 80% Light transmission
  • Even Backlit glow
Combination Printing

Mixed Opacity Zones

Strategic combination of opaque and translucent printing within single membrane. Creates dynamic lighting effects where certain graphic elements illuminate while others remain solid.

  • Variable Opacity control
  • Dynamic Lighting effects

From Concept to Installation

Our collaborative design process ensures your vision translates perfectly to architectural scale.

01

Design Brief & Inspiration

We discuss project objectives, brand guidelines, space function, and desired emotional impact. Collect reference imagery, color preferences, and technical requirements.

02

Concept Presentation

Design team presents 2-3 concept directions with moodboards and initial sketches. Includes recommendations on opaque vs. translucent printing and lighting integration strategies.

03

3D Visualization

Selected concept refined and rendered in photorealistic 3D showing exact ceiling installation. Allows evaluation of scale, proportion, and visual impact before production.

04

File Preparation & Proofing

Production-ready files prepared with bleed areas and color management. Physical print sample provided for final color approval before full production run.

05

Production & Installation

UV printing completed in 5-7 days. Membranes installed using standard STRETCH methodology‚Äîprecision tensioning ensures graphics align perfectly across seams and around fixtures.
